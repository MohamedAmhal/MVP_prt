HOW ??

AI.Marketing operates on a cashback model where users form an advertising budget, which MarketBot uses to attract potential customers through extensive advertising. The cashback service then shares the profit with users based on the sales they help generate. This approach makes the platform accessible even for those without prior marketing experience, as MarketBot handles offer selection, the launch of advertising campaigns, and provides detailed statistics​ (AI Marketing Vietnam)​.
